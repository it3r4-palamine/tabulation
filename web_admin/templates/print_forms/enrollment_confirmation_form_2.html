ENROLLMENT POLICIES & GUIDELINES:

  1. Enrollment. Every student can have at least 1 hour of session per day. Sessions maybe extended and shall be deductible to the student’s remaining hours or session credits, provided, there are time slots available. Session hours shall expire after 30 days from date of enrollment. Unused session hours shall be forfeited at expiration date. In case of partial payments, full payment shall be made within 7 days. Failure to do so shall cause the partial payment to be computed as Php 250/hr. Full payment made after the allowed 7 days shall be treated as new payment and cannot be added to the partial payment made earlier.

  2. Renewal. To maintain the reserved slot, the student shall make a full renewal payment no later than 7 days before expiration date. Failure to do so shall allow YAHSHUA Intelex to open the reserved slot to other students.

  3. Responsibilities. The parent/guardian/student is responsible in ensuring that school requirements are complied with. YAHSHUA Intelex shall only provide the training/tutorial needed to supplement the student’s learning. The parent/guardian/student is responsible in ensuring the student’s safety in going to and in leaving from the center.

  4. Training. Each enrolled student will undergo an individualized training -- a specific training program designed that suits your child's specific needs based on the result of the assessment. Also, student's training sessions will be facilitated by full -- time and well -- trained facilitators who qualified to do the YAHSHUA system.

  5. Session Goals. To make sure that all required activities for the session are done, the facilitator shall request the parent/guardian/student to first fill out the Lesson Update Form (LUF) to establish the goals for that session. After the session, the facilitator shall confirm with the parent/guardian/student the activities completed per LUF.

  6. During Student Session. Facilitators shall not allow requests to write, encode (type) and print student assignments. Students are not allowed to use YAHSHUA Intelex devices: such as laptop, tablet, printer, etc. Students should bring their own laptop, tablet etc., if necessary to make assignments, projects, etc.

  7. Reserved Time Slots. Students are given priority accommodation on their reserved time slot.

  8. After Student Session.. Students are not allowed to stay inside YAHSHUA Intelex center after session. For students with finished session but parent/guardian has not yet arrived to fetch student, facilitators shall let them sit down and wait for their parent/guardian at the lobby. Facilitators shall not accompany any student to be fetched by parent/guardian outside YAHSHUA Intelex - Stonestown building. Students with sessions in the evening should not exceed beyond 8pm. Parents/guardians should already be at the lobby not later than 8pm to fetch their child.

  9. Liability Disclaimer.. YAHSHUA Intelex shall not be held responsible for any loss of personal belongings/valuable items of students like: cellular phones, tablet, laptop, calculator, etc. while on session at our center. Each student shall be responsible to taking care of their own belongings.
